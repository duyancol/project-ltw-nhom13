It may sound incredible, but you can accurately count the exact number of grapes to produce a perfect bottle of wine. Accordingly, we need about 600-800 grapes (equivalent 11 bunches with each grape weighing about 2g, on average a bunch of grapes with 50-80 fruits) to make a bottle of 750ml wine. Thus, each ton of grapes will produce 2 barrels of 60 gallon wine , equivalent to about 720 bottles of 750ml wine.
